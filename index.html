Churn slowing down your growth?

You want your SaaS business to grow to be as big as possible. You've made promises to investors, co-founders, and maybe even yourself.

But no matter how many new customers you acquire, your churn slows your growth to painful levels. You're fighting an uphill battle.

No churn - no problems

You want every hard-earned customer that your team closes to stay a customer. You want them to have a great experience with your product and use it regularly. You want them to stay on board as a customer until their acquisition cost is paid back and they become profitable. And if a customer quits, you don't want it to counteract all the hard work your marketing and sales team is doing to close new business.

We can help lower your churn

We can help you identify the type of churn you're experiencing, its causes, and give you recommendations on how to fix it. We can even implement the recommendations for you, if you don't have the resources on hand.

30% of SaaS companies have an unacceptable level of churn*. Does yours?

*According to the Pacific Crest Private SaaS Company Survey

Services

Engagement Analysis

We can tell you what's different between customers that churn and those that haven't: what features they use, what channels they came from, how much they paid, etc.

Churn Strategy

We'll baseline and identify which of the four types of churn to reduce, collect and analyze your customer data, and make recommendations on how to lower your churn.

Stategy Implementation

We'll implement our recommendations, and make sure you can track the strategy's ROI.
