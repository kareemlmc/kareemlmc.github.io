What you get when we work together

Here's exactly what you get when you work with us.

Understand your market
We'll start by understanding your business's context: what the market is, who your ideal customer is, what their problems and motivations are, and what the solution could be.

Customer research
We'll ideally interview some customers to deep dive into the problem they're having what a good solution looks like.

Mockups
We'll translate your vision and the customer interview data into product mockups for you to review. We don't start coding until you think the mockups make sense.

Build and iterate
Once the mockups are approved, we'll build out key product features. We work in short iterations -- usually two weeks -- so you can provide feedback to inform the next set of features.

Set up reporting
Once the product is done, we'll make sure reports are set up so you can measure key business metrics.

Handoff the product
Finally, we'll transition the product to your internal team, or maintain it for you while you hire a team. All your code is robust, tested, and maintanable. Plus, we're available to help your team so the transition is as painless as possible.
